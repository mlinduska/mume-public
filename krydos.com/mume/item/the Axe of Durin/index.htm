the Axe of Durin

EXAMINE:
This wonderful dwarven great axe was once wielded by Durin the Deathless, the father and King of the dwarves of Moria. Forged in the mightiest of their great, ancient furnaces, it emanates awe and power. The shaft is shod with truesilver, and both the hilt and the knob at the top shine with the purest of gold. On either side of the top, two huge blades leave you wondering how anyone, save Aule himself, could ever create such a truly magnificent masterpiece of craftsmanship.
